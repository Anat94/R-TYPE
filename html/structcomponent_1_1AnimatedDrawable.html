pathpath to the spritesheet
nbSpritesnumber of sprites to load (ex: {10, 1} for ten sprites on 1 line)
spriteSizesize of the sprites in the spritesheet (ex: {34, 12} for a 34 by 12 sprite)
gapssize of the horizontal and vertical gaps in the spritesheet (ex: {1, 3} for a 1 pixel gap in the x-axis, and a 3 pixel gap in the y-axis)
firstOffsetsize of the first offset of the sprite (ex: {101, 4} if the sprite you want start at 101 pixels in the x-axis, and 4 pixels in the y-axis)
curretnIdxcurrent index of the texture of the sprite (ex: {2, 0} to start at the third sprite of the spritesheet)
